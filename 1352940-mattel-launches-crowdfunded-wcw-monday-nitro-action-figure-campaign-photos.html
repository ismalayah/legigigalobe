Mattel Launches Crowdfunded WCW Monday Nitro Action Figure Campaign (Photos)

May 01, 2024
blog

If you’re a diehard wrestling action figure collector and loved WCW Monday Nitro then you’ll love the new Mattel WCW Monday Nitro campaign!

Mattel Launches their new crowdfunded WCW Monday Nitro stage + figures

Today, Mattel’s Manager of Global Brand Marketing – Action Figures, Steve Ozer, revealed the news of Mattel’s new crowdfunded WCW Monday Nitro stage and action figure bundle! This is similar to the New Gen Arena they launched as their first crowdfunded project, which was fully backed.

This year’s, launching this Wednesday, March 29th at 9am/PST, features the WCW Monday Nitro entrance stage along with Ultimate Edition action figures of:

  • Hulk Hogan (Early Funding Bonus if 5,000 Backers by April 7th)
  • Rey Mysterio (5,000 Backers w/ Stage)
  • Diamond Dallas Page (7,000 Backers w/ Hogan (if met by 4/7), Rey, Stage)
  • Scott Steiner (9,000 Backers w/ Hogan (if met by 4/7), Rey, DDP, Stage)
  • Ultimate Warrior WCW Debut – (11,000 Backers w/ Hogan (if met by 4/7), Rey, DDP, Steiner, Stage)

This campaign launches and has a value of on it. Funding for the project . If you were unaware, websites offer payment plan options where you can Pay-in-Four payments over two months, such as PayPal, Klarna, Affirm and Shop to name a few.
