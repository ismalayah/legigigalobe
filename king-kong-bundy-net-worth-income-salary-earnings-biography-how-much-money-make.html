Christopher Alan “Chris” Pallies, best known by his ring moniker King Kong Bundy, is an American professional wrestler, stand-up comedian, and actor. Bundy rose to prominence in the World Wrestling Federation (WWF, now WWE) during the 1980s and 1990s: he was the fourth victim of The Undertaker’s famous 21-match undefeated WrestleMania streak at WrestleMania XI in 1995, and he headlined WrestleMania 2 in 1986 against Hulk Hogan in a steel cage match for the WWF Championship. Bundy was nicknamed “The Walking Condominium” by WWF play-by-play announcer Gorilla Monsoon due to his stature.
