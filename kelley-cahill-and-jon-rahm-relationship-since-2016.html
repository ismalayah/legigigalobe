You might recognize Jon Rahm as one of the top professional golfers in the world. In addition to this, you might also know all the stats of the 2020 world number 1 but do you know that he has a wife named Kelley Cahill. 

In similar fashion, the pair have become golf’s hottest couple. Before tying the knot in 2019, they made waves because of their chemistry off the field, additionally, Kelley’s immense popularity on social media also helped them become the most popular couple in golf. 

Jon Rahm And Kelley Cahill’s Relationship 

Kelley Cahill and met while both of them were still in college, in fact, she was one of the best members of the track and field team at Arizona State and he was also involved in sports at the college. 

The pair connected instantly due to their interesting sports. As a matter of fact, they started having a friendly competition between the two of them. 

Cahill and Jon compete in various sports, Kelley also claims that she is the better among the two at javelin throwing. 

Their competitive nature almost jeopardized their relationship as they fell out after an intense tennis match and have not played tennis against each other ever since.

Rahm And Cahill Are Married

As mentioned above, Cahill and Rahm met at Arizona State University. After a while of courtship, they started dating. 

Eventually, they became really close and after two years decided to get married. 

They tied the knot in December 2019, they held a romantic ceremony in a quaint place near Rahm's hometown of Barrika. 

In November 2020, Jon announced via an Instagram post that he and his wife were expecting their first child.

The couple lives in Arizona and is the biggest couple in golf at the moment, in fact, some outlets have even called them the new faces of the game.

Jon Rahm Is A Rising Golf Star

Rahm became the top golfer in the world in 2021, similarly, he has also won several tournaments and is a popular icon of the game. Some of his success is also due to his wife’s support. 

He is called Rahmbo because his name is similar to the legendary Sylvester Stallone character, John Rambo of the popular action film series, “Rambo”. 

The star also became immensely popular after he won the BMW Championship in 2020 against United States’ Dustin Johnson with a birdie in the first extra hole.
